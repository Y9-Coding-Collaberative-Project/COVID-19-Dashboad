What is Coronavirus

The Coronavirus (COVID-19, standing for for Corona Virus Diease from the year 2019) and is a virus affecting respiratory functions similar to SARS (severe acute resperatory syndrome). However this virus is much less lethal with only a 0.7-3.4 lethality rate. It is an interspecies virus.
